Typography.

The site is set in a small, carefully chosen palette of three families. Each carries a specific job: display, text, and metadata.

Display — Instrument Serif

Regular · Italic

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Designed for moments that deserve a deep breath.

Text — Inter

300 · 400 · 500 · 600

Good typography is invisible. It carries the reader through the page without announcing itself, and it leaves room for the writing to do the work it was written to do. We choose a workhorse typeface for body text the way a carpenter chooses a saw — for how it cuts, not how it looks on the wall.

Inter sets at 16px with comfortable leading and a slight tightening of letter-spacing at larger sizes. Numerals are tabular, so financial figures align across rows.

300 — Light · the patient first reading

400 — Regular · the working weight

500 — Medium · for emphasis in dense lists

600 — Semibold · only headings and labels
